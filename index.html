Banana Chocolate Chip Pancake

Here's how to make the most delicious healthy pancake with simple ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 1 bananas
  • 2 eggs
  • 3/4 cups of oatmeal
  • as much chocolate chips as you want
  • vanilla
  • brown sugar
  • butter and olive oil

Instructions:

  1. blender
  2. plate for the pancakes
  3. spatula to flip the pancakes
  4. pan
  5. blend all the ingredients together except the butter and oil
  6. heat pan and put the oil and olive oil
  7. make disk shape of any size for your pancake
